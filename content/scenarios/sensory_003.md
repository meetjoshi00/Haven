## Setting

You are planning a day out with a close friend. They have suggested a busy indoor market, which you know would be overwhelming for you. You want to be honest without making them feel bad.

## What to practise

Disclosing a sensory need to a trusted friend and suggesting an alternative. Focus on: being honest without over-apologising, proposing a workable alternative, and keeping the conversation collaborative.

## The scenario

Your friend has just enthusiastically suggested the market for Saturday.

## Setting

You have just started a new job and are stuck on a part of the spreadsheet system the team uses. A friendly colleague is nearby and you want to ask for help after attempting it yourself first.

## What to practise

Asking a colleague for help on a specific task without being too vague or demanding. Focus on: identifying the specific problem, framing it as a question not a complaint, and thanking them for their time.

## The scenario

A colleague greets you and asks how you are settling in — your opening to ask for help.

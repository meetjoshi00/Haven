## Setting

You are talking with a friend who has just stated an opinion you strongly disagree with. They seem confident in their view. The conversation is informal but you need to assert a different position without damaging the friendship.

## What to practise

Disagreeing respectfully while maintaining the relationship. Focus on: acknowledging the other person's view before presenting your own, using "I" language, and staying calm even if they push back.

## The scenario

Your friend has just made a strong statement about remote work.

## Setting

In a team meeting your manager has announced a process change you believe will cause problems. You have data to support your concern. The meeting is closing and you need to raise this now.

## What to practise

Raising a professional objection to a manager's decision respectfully and constructively. Focus on: framing it around practical impact (not personal opinion), offering an alternative, and doing it before the decision is finalised.

## The scenario

The manager is about to close the meeting and asks for final questions.

## Setting

A colleague has asked you to review their draft presentation before it goes to a client. You have noticed real issues with the data section and conclusion. They are proud of the work.

## What to practise

Delivering honest, constructive feedback diplomatically. Focus on: acknowledging what works before raising issues, framing problems around the client's needs not personal taste, and inviting a collaborative response.

## The scenario

Your colleague asks what you think and mentions how hard they worked on it.

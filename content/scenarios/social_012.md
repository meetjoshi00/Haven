## Setting

You are at a friend's dinner party. You want to compliment their beautifully decorated home. Later they will compliment your jacket and you will need to respond naturally.

## What to practise

Giving a specific, genuine compliment and receiving one graciously. Focus on: being specific (not just "nice"), sounding natural rather than scripted, and accepting compliments without excessive deflection.

## The scenario

Your friend asks how you're finding the place — a natural opening to compliment the décor.

## Setting

It has been a loud, overwhelming week in the open-plan office. A colleague has noticed you seem stressed and is checking in with you.

## What to practise

Explaining sensory overload to a colleague in a work-appropriate way. Focus on: being honest without over-sharing, naming what is happening for you, and accepting the concern gracefully.

## The scenario

Your colleague has noticed you seem stressed and asks if you are okay.

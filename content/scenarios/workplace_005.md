## Setting

You have been added to a project team mid-way through the project. They are in a meeting reviewing progress. Your manager introduced you briefly and has left. You need to integrate without disrupting the flow.

## What to practise

Joining an established team gracefully. Focus on: signalling readiness to contribute without demanding attention, asking a useful question that shows preparation, and not asking the team to start over for you.

## The scenario

The team asks if you have had a chance to look at the brief — your opening to join in.

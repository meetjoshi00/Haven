## Setting

You have been at your job six months. You have an autism diagnosis and are formally requesting workplace accommodations from HR: written meeting agendas, two WFH days per week, and a quiet workspace.

## What to practise

Making a formal accommodation request in a professional HR meeting. Focus on: disclosing clearly if appropriate, naming specific adjustments (not vague needs), and referencing your legal right to reasonable adjustments.

## The scenario

The HR representative opens the meeting and confirms you wanted to discuss working arrangements.

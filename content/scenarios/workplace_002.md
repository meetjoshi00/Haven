## Setting

It is the first week of a new school term. You are sitting next to someone you have not met during a group activity. The teacher has asked everyone to introduce themselves.

## What to practise

Introducing yourself to someone new in a relaxed academic setting. Focus on: giving your name, asking at least one follow-up question, and leaving a natural pause for the other person to respond.

## The scenario

The person next to you introduces themselves first — now it is your turn to respond and continue the conversation.

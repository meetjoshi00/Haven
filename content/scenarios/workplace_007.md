## Setting

Your manager is giving you direct criticism about errors in a report you submitted that had to be corrected before distribution.

## What to practise

Receiving criticism professionally — without getting defensive or going silent. Focus on: thanking them for the feedback, asking a clarifying question, and signalling you intend to learn from it.

## The scenario

Your manager opens the conversation by telling you there were errors in your report.

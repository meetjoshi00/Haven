## Setting

You are at the customer service desk of a shop. You bought a pair of headphones last week that stopped working. You have the receipt. The shop assistant is polite but busy and will ask questions before processing the return.

## What to practise

Calmly and clearly explaining a problem and asserting your right to a return. Focus on: stating the issue specifically, staying polite even if frustrated, and providing the information they need (receipt, fault description).

## The scenario

You approach the customer service desk with the headphones and receipt.

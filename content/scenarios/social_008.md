## Setting

You are at an informal social gathering. Two people you know are chatting about a film you have also seen. There is a natural pause in their conversation and you would like to join in.

## What to practise

Entering a conversation smoothly without interrupting. Focus on: waiting for the right moment, acknowledging what was already said, and contributing without taking over.

## The scenario

There is a brief pause in the conversation about the film — your opening to join in.

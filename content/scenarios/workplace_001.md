## Setting

Your teacher has finished a lesson and most students have left. You did not understand part of the explanation and want to ask for clarification before the next lesson builds on it.

## What to practise

Asking an authority figure a specific, focused question. Focus on: identifying the specific part you did not understand, asking clearly without being too vague, and being polite without over-apologising.

## The scenario

The teacher notices you waiting and asks if you have a question.

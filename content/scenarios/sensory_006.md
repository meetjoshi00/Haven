## Setting

You are doing an escape room activity with colleagues. The environment is very stimulating — loud, bright, busy. You are reaching sensory overload and need a short break outside.

## What to practise

Requesting a brief sensory break in a group activity without over-explaining or making a big deal of it. Focus on: being direct, keeping it brief, and reassuring the group you will return.

## The scenario

The group is energised and pushing hard to solve the puzzle. You need to step away for a moment.

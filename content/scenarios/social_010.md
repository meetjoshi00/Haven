## Setting

A friend has invited you to a loud, crowded party on Saturday. You do not want to go. Your friend is enthusiastic and is pushing for you to say yes.

## What to practise

Declining a social invitation clearly and kindly without over-explaining. Focus on: saying no clearly (not "maybe"), thanking them for the invite, and keeping the friendship positive.

## The scenario

Your friend is pressuring you to commit to the party.

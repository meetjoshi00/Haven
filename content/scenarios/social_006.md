## Setting

You are in a park on a warm day. A bench has one person sitting at one end with bags next to them. All other benches are taken. The person is reading and has not looked up.

## What to practise

Politely asking a stranger if you can share their space. Focus on: using a clear, polite request, explaining the situation briefly, and accepting their response gracefully.

## The scenario

You approach the bench and need to get the person's attention before asking.

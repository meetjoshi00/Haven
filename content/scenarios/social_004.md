## Setting

You are leaving your home and encounter your neighbour at their front door. You have seen them a few times but have never had a proper conversation. They notice you and smile.

## What to practise

Exchanging a brief, friendly greeting with a neighbour. Focus on: responding warmly to a greeting, adding a small conversational contribution, and closing the exchange naturally.

## The scenario

Your neighbour spots you as you both step out at the same time.

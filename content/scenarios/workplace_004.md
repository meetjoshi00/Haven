## Setting

A report is due on Friday and you will not be ready to the required standard due to an unexpected urgent task. You want to request a short extension before the deadline.

## What to practise

Requesting a deadline extension proactively and professionally. Focus on: raising it early (not after missing it), briefly explaining why without over-explaining, and proposing a specific new date.

## The scenario

Your manager catches you looking stressed and asks what is on your mind.

## Setting

You arrive at a restaurant. The host is about to seat you next to the kitchen and a loud speaker. Other tables are available.

## What to practise

Politely requesting a different table based on a sensory need. Focus on: stating the need without over-explaining, making a specific alternative request, and staying warm towards the host.

## The scenario

The host begins leading you to the loud table near the kitchen.

## Setting

You are in a park trying to read. Someone nearby is playing music on a portable speaker at a volume that makes it hard to concentrate. They seem unaware.

## What to practise

Calmly asking a stranger to adjust a behaviour that is affecting your sensory comfort. Focus on: addressing them directly, making a specific and reasonable request, and thanking them afterwards.

## The scenario

The person with the speaker glances over and notices you looking their way.

## Setting

You are a student meeting your teacher after class to explain that the flickering fluorescent lighting in the classroom gives you headaches and makes it hard to concentrate. You want to request a solution.

## What to practise

Clearly explaining a sensory need to an authority figure and requesting reasonable accommodation. Focus on: being specific about the issue, using a calm and professional tone, and proposing a solution rather than just stating the problem.

## The scenario

Your teacher has agreed to speak with you after class.

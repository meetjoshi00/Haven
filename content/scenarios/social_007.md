## Setting

You are calling your GP surgery to book an appointment for a check-up. The receptionist answers. Phone calls have no visual cues — communicate clearly through speech alone.

## What to practise

Making a clear, organised phone call to book an appointment. Focus on: introducing yourself, stating your request clearly, and providing the information the receptionist needs without them having to ask repeatedly.

## The scenario

The phone is answered by the receptionist at your GP surgery.

## Setting

You are at a crowded after-work event. A colleague keeps standing too close during conversation, unaware that it is causing you sensory discomfort.

## What to practise

Requesting personal space from a colleague without damaging the relationship. Focus on: making the request matter-of-factly without blaming them, briefly explaining your need, and continuing the conversation naturally.

## The scenario

The colleague is chatting to you, standing closer than is comfortable.

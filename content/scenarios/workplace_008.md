## Setting

You are in a job interview for a junior data analyst role. The interviewer has welcomed you and opened with the standard "tell me about yourself" question.

## What to practise

Giving a strong, concise professional introduction. Focus on: opening with your relevant background (not your life story), connecting your experience to the role, and ending with why you applied.

## The scenario

The interviewer settles in and asks you to tell them about yourself.

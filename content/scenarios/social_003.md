## Setting

Someone has just helped you pick up items you dropped in a shopping centre. They have handed everything back to you and are smiling, waiting for you to respond.

## What to practise

Expressing genuine gratitude in a brief, warm social exchange. Focus on: acknowledging the help directly, using warm language, and leaving the interaction on a positive note.

## The scenario

The helpful stranger hands back your last dropped item and smiles at you.

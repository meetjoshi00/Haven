## Setting

You have just started a new job. The open-plan office is overwhelming. You have scheduled a meeting with your line manager to formally request a desk move and permission to use noise-cancelling headphones.

## What to practise

Making a formal workplace accommodation request clearly and professionally. Focus on: naming the specific adjustments needed, framing it as a reasonable adjustment rather than a complaint, and being clear that it affects your ability to do your best work.

## The scenario

Your line manager greets you at the start of the meeting.

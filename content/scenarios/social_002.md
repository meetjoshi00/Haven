## Setting

You are on a street in an unfamiliar area and need to find the local library. A stranger is walking by at a comfortable pace — they look approachable and not in a hurry.

## What to practise

Politely stopping a stranger to ask for help. Focus on: using a polite opener to get attention, being clear about what you need, and thanking them afterwards.

## The scenario

The stranger notices you looking around and offers to help.

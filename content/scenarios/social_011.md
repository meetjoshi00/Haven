## Setting

You are in a supermarket queue. Someone has joined in front of you, apparently not noticing you were there. Other people are watching. The situation is awkward but the other person seems genuinely unaware.

## What to practise

Addressing a misunderstanding calmly without escalating or backing down. Focus on: assuming good intent, speaking clearly but without aggression, and resolving it with minimal drama.

## The scenario

The person who stepped in front turns and notices your expression.
